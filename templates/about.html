About DellVE Benchmark

Dellve Deep is a specialized performance benchmark for general purpose graphics processing unit subsystems in high performance computing servers. In the construction of our project, we leverage a number of different tools and technologies, namely the Python and Javascript programming languages, the cuDNN Deep Neural Net (DNN) library, and open source projects such as Netdata, Click, and Falcon.
